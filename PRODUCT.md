# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Owners, operators, and sales, support, customer-success, and operations leaders at small and midsize businesses. They are curious about AI but skeptical of broad promises, need to identify a useful first automation, and want faster response and cleaner handoffs without learning a complex automation platform.

## Product Purpose

Bamboo AI turns an anonymous website visitor into a business-specific AI agent blueprint and a qualified next step. The funnel lets a visitor configure and preview a useful agent before providing contact information, then routes qualified interest toward an optimization call or launch plan.

Success means a new visitor understands the offer in seconds, experiences a real input-to-outcome product loop, completes the builder, saves a valuable blueprint, and knows exactly what happens next.

## Brand Personality

Engineered, alive, decisive. The voice is concise, concrete, calm, and commercially confident. It explains business outcomes in normal language and earns trust through visible product behavior.

## Anti-references

- Generic neon-on-black AI landing pages.
- Purple gradients, glowing orbs, glass-card stacks, and particle fields.
- Editorial serif and tiny-mono-label styling used as a shortcut for sophistication.
- Literal bamboo forests, leaves, eco-spa styling, mascot robots, and illustrated brains.
- Fake proof, invented metrics, internal placeholders, and vague transformation claims.
- Long gated forms that ask for contact details before showing value.

## Design Principles

1. Show the complete visitor-to-outcome loop before explaining features.
2. Let visitors build value before asking for contact information.
3. Make every signal, line, status, and animation explain system behavior.
4. Use confidence, guardrails, and human handoff to make automation trustworthy.
5. Keep one obvious primary action and one honest next step on every route.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA. Support keyboard operation, visible focus, 44px touch targets, reduced motion, 200% zoom, high-contrast body and placeholder text, semantic form errors, safe-area insets, and status cues that do not rely on color alone.
